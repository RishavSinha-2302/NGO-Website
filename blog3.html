Funding and Sustainability: Building the Future

By Mr. Sanket Landge, Operations and Fundraising Manager

Strategic Funding for a Lasting Impact

At SHIKSHA SETU, we believe that a vision as powerful as educating underprivileged children deserves not only passion, but also a foundation of financial stability. Sustainable funding isn’t just about keeping our programs running — it’s about scaling our impact and reaching every child who needs us.

Our strategy is intentionally diverse. We engage with a mix of individual donors, corporate partners, grant-making organizations, and government bodies. Each contributes uniquely, helping us balance immediate needs with long-term goals. We never rely on one source alone — instead, we cultivate a portfolio that is dynamic, resilient, and aligned with our mission.

The Heartbeat: Donor Relationships and Community

Behind every contribution is a story of trust. We see our donors as true partners in our journey. By maintaining transparent communication, regular updates, and measurable results, we nurture relationships that go beyond transactions. Donors want to feel connected to the change they’re enabling — and we make sure they do.

Our local communities, too, play a vital role. Whether through volunteering, spreading awareness, or even modest financial support, their involvement adds strength and legitimacy to our work. Community support is more than emotional — it's a tangible, driving force.

Innovation: Social Enterprises and Impact Investment

In today’s world, innovation is key to staying sustainable. We are exploring and integrating models like social enterprises — mission-driven ventures that generate income while solving social challenges. These not only support our operations financially but also empower beneficiaries through skill-building and employment.

We’re also laying the groundwork for impact investment opportunities. These partnerships attract socially conscious investors who seek both financial returns and measurable social good. It's a promising frontier that aligns perfectly with our values.

Looking Ahead

The path to sustainability is not always linear, but it is always essential. At SHIKSHA SETU, we are committed to evolving our funding practices with creativity, transparency, and integrity.

Together with our partners, supporters, and community champions, we’re not just sustaining a mission — we’re building a future.

– Mr. Sanket Landge
Operations and Fundraising Manager, SHIKSHA SETU
