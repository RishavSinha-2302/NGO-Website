How We Work: Insights from the Head of Programs

By Mr. Rishav Sinha, Head of Programs and Outreach

Structured for Impact

At SHIKSHA SETU, our programs are designed with one goal in mind — to deliver measurable and meaningful educational support to underprivileged children. As the Head of Programs and Outreach, I am proud to share how our well-defined structures and strategies drive our mission forward.

Each program begins with a needs assessment phase, where we engage with local communities to understand the specific challenges children face in accessing education. From there, we design custom interventions — from after-school support and mobile learning units to digital literacy sessions and mentorship initiatives.

Smart Resource Management

Managing resources effectively is the backbone of our success. Our team closely monitors the allocation and usage of every resource — whether it's school supplies, volunteers' time, or digital tools. We use technology to track distribution and ensure transparency.

Collaboration is key. By partnering with local schools, volunteers, corporate sponsors, and government bodies, we’re able to scale our reach and optimize impact without duplicating efforts. Our coordination model encourages community participation and accountability.

Core Activities and Outcomes

Our flagship activities include:

  • Community Learning Hubs in low-income areas
  • Mobile libraries and digital classrooms
  • Mentorship programs connecting students with professionals
  • Workshops on health, hygiene, and soft skills

These initiatives are not just about imparting knowledge, but about building confidence and creating lasting change. We've seen children go from dropping out of school to excelling in academics — all because of consistent support and encouragement.

Our Way Forward

We are constantly learning and adapting. Regular feedback from the field helps us improve our strategies and introduce new tools that make learning more accessible and engaging. Our team is committed to staying agile and community-focused.

Every child reached, every milestone achieved, and every story of transformation fuels our motivation. Together, with our partners and supporters, we aim to expand our footprint and bring quality education to even more corners of the country.

– Mr. Rishav Sinha
Head of Programs and Outreach, SHIKSHA SETU
